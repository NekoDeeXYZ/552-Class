Aspirations of A Supportive Librarian

"Great Books help you understand, and they help you feel understood" -John Green

My forever goal will always be to help as many people feel valued and heard. That they matter.

Professionally my currently goal is to provide the teens of my local community valuable experiences in building skills and relationships within the community.

I also aspire to help the teens of the community realize that they too make an impact and a difference in the community around them. That they are seen and heard in my library.

Delany McCarthy-Plut
