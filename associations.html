My Professional Associations

Current Memberships:

American Libraries Association

The ALA is the go-to organization for all things library in the United States. They offer plenty of advice and support for everything and anything related to libraries. From articles by experts, practice suggestions, education, trainings and even mentorship ALA is a core professional organization for any librarian.

Portage County District Libraries

This is where I work! There are plenty of great people working and volunteering here. The organization is very friendly, and team focused. My time here has given me great insight and experience in the world of public libraries.
