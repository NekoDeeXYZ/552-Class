2023 $5 or less Horror Gametober Challenge

Week One: Horror From the 2010s

Kick Back and revist the more interesting horrors from the recent past. This games where once big hits, and offer more than one can expect for practically free games!

Cry of Fear

Straight from 2013, Cry of Fear is set inside a deserted town filled with madness and horrific creatures. Blast away any enimies that stand in your way and try to figure out just what is happening. With multiple endings, unlockable characters and even a multiplayer campaign this game still has a great amount of content.

Sally Face - Episode One

Explore the world of sally, a young boy with a prosthetic face. Will you be able to wade through this tragic mystery to uncover the hard trueths of his world?

Doki Doki Literature Club!

Join the Doki Doki Literature Club and make dear friends you can charish each and everyday. This game is best served going blind but it is not quite as cute, happy or friendly as it seems. In fact, Doki Doki literature Club is a bit more disterbing then you may expect.

Spooky's Jump Scare Mansion

Tryy and beat Spooky's Mansion filled with 1,000 rooms of nothing but cute horror. There is definelty nothing that will harm you or try to eat you or steal your soul. Afterall it is owned by a cute little ghost girl.

Week Two: Childlike Horror

Nothing is creeper then the stuff we saw as kids. Well except these games, they just make it worse.

Five Nights At Freddy's

Take a job at an abondended Pizzaria they said, It will be easy they said. You know what they did not tell you? That the animatronics want to eat you alive.

Dark Deception

It's like Pacman, except you are pacman. Exept the maze is really creepy. Oh and if you dont run the monsters really will kill you. Run through horrifing mazes while being chases by monsters that just want to eat you.

Free to play

Pony Island

Boot up a game of Pony Island, a simple arcade game about ponies. Well not quite, as you soon will find yourself trapped in the very machine, hurridly solving puzzles and fixing code to escape with your life and soul.

Free to play

Paper Lily Prologue

Don't Let the title mislead you, Paper Lily Prologue is a full standalone game. In this game you follow Kat as she makes friends, solves puzzles and explores in seemingly old school building. In this game puzzles have multiple solutions all leading to vastly different endings.

Free to play

Week Three: Graphically Graphic

I'm not saying these were picked for thier graphics, but that may of also been a factor. The games this week pairs interesting visual design and music with graphic storytelling to give us some rather horrifing experiences.

Tiny Bunny: Prologue

A short but visually dense game with oppressing and cold soundtrack is set in a quiet village in the Siberian Forest. In this game you follow Anton as he settles in the small village, discovering the horrifing secrets of the village as terrifying events unfold before him.

The Supper

In this imagaitive story featuring almost cuddly characters and a custom soundtrack you play as the sweet and kind Ms.Appleton as she puts together a special meal for special customers.

My Friend is a Raven

In this visually unique game, you play as a chararcter at the end of the world as he returns one last time to his home. This game while short does feature multiple endings and one elusive and very sick raven.

If On A Winter's Night, Four Travelers

A bit less of a horror, but entirely dark, If on a Winter's Night, Four Travelers pairs a detailed intricate world with amazing atmsphere to fully bring the characters to life. In this game youi explore not one but four character's stories in a narattive that perfectly captures all levels of human emotion.

Week Four: Horror With Friends!

For this final week let's gather some of our closest friends and all get scared togther. Afterall, Horror is more fun if you with others!

Friday the 13th: The Game

Will you kill or be killed with your friends? Take on the classic serial killer Jason Voorhees, in the very Camp Crystal Lake from the movies.

$4.99

PROJECT: PLAYTIME

Meet back up with your friends in this game within the world of Poppy's Playtime. In this asymetric horror game six players will try and build one gaint toy together while a seventh player will control a monster set out to detroy these tresspassers.

SCP: Containment Breach Multiplayer

Connecting back to the ever expanding SCP Foundation. In this multiplayer extension up to 64 players can explore wreck havoc within the facility. Can you escape the SCP foundation with your friends? Will you play as an entiety, a guard or personel? Together will you be able to escape or even lock down the facility?

We were Here

You and your friend find yourselves trapped inside a cold abondeoned castle with nothing but each other and a weal walkie talkie to talk through, will you two be able to help each other out in time? Or will communication fall through leaving you each to freeze alone?
