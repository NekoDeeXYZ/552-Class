Week One: Horror From the 2010s

Kick Back and revist some classic horrors from the past decade

Silent Hill Homecoming

A Classic game based off the classic horror movie series Silent Hill. Play as a father searching through a foggy odd town for his missing daughter, but there are many things within this town who are hunting him as well.

Slender: The Arrival

Slenderman, one of the many horrors the internet has created, but also the main threat in a huge range of games from the early 2010s. Will you be able to survive this encounter with him?

Corpse Party

A grimy gorey game not for those who faint at the sight of blood.

Amnesia: The Dark Desent

Utalizing the classic case of amnesia, play as Daniel a man with muddled memories inside an eerie castle. But you are not alone, and the horrors found in these halls are sure to keep you up at night.

Week Two: Childlike Horror

Nothing is creeper then the stuff we saw as kids. Well except these games, they just make it worse.

Five Nights At Freddy's

Take a job at an abondended Pizzaria they said, It will be easy they said. You know what they did not tell you? That the animatronics want to eat you alive.

Among the Sleep

Waking up along at night is much more scary as a child. Relive that specific fear in Among the Sleep, playing as a child as you wonder through a nightmarish land looking for your mom.

Tattletail

Did Furbies freak you out as a kid? Have your worst fears confirmed in Tattletail.

Smile For Me

A Colorful and Goofy game with bright visuals and just the slightest touch of eeriness one expects from late night television.
