2023 Horror Gametober Challenge

Week One: Horror From the 2010s

Kick Back and revist some classic horrors from the past decade

Silent Hill Homecoming

A Classic game based off the classic horror movie series Silent Hill. Play as a father searching through a foggy odd town for his missing daughter, but there are many things within this town who are hunting him as well.

Slender: The Arrival

Slenderman, one of the many horrors the internet has created, but also the main threat in a huge range of games from the early 2010s. Will you be able to survive this encounter with him?

Corpse Party

A grimy gorey game not for those who faint at the sight of blood.

Amnesia: The Dark Desent

Utalizing the classic case of amnesia, play as Daniel a man with muddled memories inside an eerie castle. But you are not alone, and the horrors found in these halls are sure to keep you up at night.

Week Two: Childlike Horror

Nothing is creeper then the stuff we saw as kids. Well except these games, they just make it worse.

Five Nights At Freddy's

Take a job at an abondended Pizzaria they said, It will be easy they said. You know what they did not tell you? That the animatronics want to eat you alive.

Among the Sleep

Waking up along at night is much more scary as a child. Relive that specific fear in Among the Sleep, playing as a child as you wonder through a nightmarish land looking for your mom.

Tattletail

Did Furbies freak you out as a kid? Have your worst fears confirmed in Tattletail.

Smile For Me

A Colorful and Goofy game with bright visuals and just the slightest touch of eeriness one expects from late night television.

Week Three: Graphically Graphic

I'm not saying these were picked for thier graphics, but that may of also been a factor. The games this week pairs interesting visual design and music with graphic storytelling to give us some rather horrifing experiences.

OMORI

Omori cleverly mixes a variety of mediums to deliver an experience you won't easily forget. In this game you play as a teenage boy with an alter ego names Omori, and through them the player will explore both thier worlds to discover his forgotton past.

Firework

In this slow paced narrative with increasingly creepy and immersive visuals and music, we follow a rookie police officer in a chinese village as he slowly investigaes a closed case massacre.

OxenFree

Although not quite a horror, OxenFree features a story rich atmosphere with just enough creepy vibes that will send shivers down your spine. Oxenfree is about a group oif teens who travel to an abondended island for a classic overnight teen party, that goes horribly wrong.

Darkwood

Darkwood is a survival horror that will increasingly put you on edge as you play it. With eerie and atmospheric music and sound combined with limited field of view darkwood creates an anguishing tense experience.

Week Four: Horror With Friends!

For this final week let's gather some of our closest friends and all get scared togther. Afterall, Horror is more fun if you with others!

The Blackout Club

At night your peaceful town becomes strange... all the adults act wierd and are seemingly controled by an unknown entity? Strange things are happening here, and with up to three of your friends can you solve the mysteries or will you fall subject to the entity.

Deceit

In this game you and five others are thrown into a game of deceit, death and puzzles. Some of the fellow players are there to kill you, others are there to also escape. Equipped with a gun and few tasks to go off of, who will trust and can you escape?

SCP: Secret Laboratory

Taking the orginal SCP Containment Breach game to new levels. In this iteration of players can choose to play more than just the D-Class personal but instead play as guards or even the anamolies themselves!

GTFO

Grab up to three friends and try once again to survive in this deadly undground prison. We all know how hard this game is, an if you are like me this game was raged quit so fast. But with that price tag if you have it, it's about time you give it another play.
